Activity 1: What is Law?
While reading the Gearty (2019) article, keep in mind the following question: what is the point that Gearty is trying to make?

Part 1
The passage emphasises the significance of the rule of law in maintaining order and settling disputes within societies. It traces the evolution of governance from monarchs and priests to the establishment of laws based on mutually agreed constraints on power. The author highlights the importance of legal frameworks in shaping societal conduct, stressing the role of written words and undertakings (laws) in holding individuals accountable, even the most powerful ones. The author acknowledges the complexities and debates within the legal system, underscoring the role of reason, argument, logic, and evidence in the legal process. Ultimately, the passage underscores the value of the rule of law in preserving societal values and protecting freedom.
